Enter Your Main Idea


This is going to be the general topic of your brainstorming project. The general topic is meant to be very broad and should not be thought as of the title of your brainstorming. Instead, the general idea of your topic should be thought as a topic that all of your ideas are going to relate to. The purpose of generating the general topic first is to keep your ideas concentrated to a specific area. A few examples of brainstorming topics are: Education, Fitness, Nutrition, Business, Political Issues, Media
