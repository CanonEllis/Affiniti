Step Five

At this point you should have a bunch of different groups. If you look over your groups you may even, see that a few of them share similarities to each other. In this part of the process you can group groups into a large group which allows you to have a more defined structure. For example, the man idea Education may have 3 main groups Teaching Assistants, Students, and Professors and inside each of those groups are smaller groups that contain bite sized notes.
